About

Shree Engineering Works

Precision. Quality. Experience.

We, M/s Shree Engineering Works, are a certified manufacturing industry specializing in industrial fabrication and auto body work.

Over the last 12 years, we have worked with numerous engineering and bio-product manufacturing companies. Here’s a brief overview of our infrastructure and capabilities:

  • Our manufacturing unit is located at Pandharpur Phata, Shirwal, covering 8,000 sq. ft.
  • Equipped with skilled manpower and a 15 HP 3-phase power connection.
  • Certified with GST, MSME, Udyam Aadhaar, ISO, and registered with Trade Certificate (Form 17).
